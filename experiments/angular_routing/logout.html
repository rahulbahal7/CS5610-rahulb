Explanation

The functionality related to a user is present in the UserService. The entered credentials will be passed to user service, and on success the user will be logged in. On successful login, the login form will be hidden and Profile, Logout buttons will be displayed. As expected, clicking the Logout button will Log out the user and the LogIn form will again displayed be.
